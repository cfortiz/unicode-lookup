Unicode Character Lookup

How to Use

Enter one of the following in the search box:

  • Character Name: Enter part or all of a character's official Unicode name. You can use lowercase in your search (e.g., "THORN", "hiragana").
  • Character: Enter the character itself (e.g., ᚦ, か).
  • Codepoint: Enter the codepoint in hexadecimal (e.g., U+16A6, U+304B) or decimal (e.g., 5798, 12363).
